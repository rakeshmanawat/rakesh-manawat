Dual Axis Solar Tracker

Description: A renewable energy project designed to maximize solar power generation. The tracker automatically aligns solar panels toward the direction of highest sunlight intensity, ensuring maximum energy absorption throughout the day.

Key Contributions

  • Designed a mechanism to track solar movement along both horizontal and vertical axes.
  • Increased solar panel efficiency by optimizing sunlight exposure.
  • Implemented environment-friendly and cost-effective renewable energy utilization.
  • Developed hardware control logic to adjust panel orientation in real time.
